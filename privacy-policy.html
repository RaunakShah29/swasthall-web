Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 Updated: March 2026
Swasthall is built on the principle that your health data belongs to you. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and your full rights over it. We never sell your health data.

📋 Who This Applies To

This policy applies to all Swasthall users: Patients, Doctors, Nurses, Pharmacists, Lab Technicians, and Hospital / Clinic Administrators.

📦 Information We Collect

•Personal info: full name, email, phone, date of birth, gender, profile photo
•Health info: medical history, medications, allergies, lab results, consultation notes
•Location: only when you use Emergency features — with your explicit permission
•Professional info (providers): NMC number, degree, insurance details, certifications
•Financial: transaction history — we do NOT store card or wallet credentials
•Usage data: app activity, consultation timestamps, language preference

✅ How We Use Your Data

✓Connect you with verified healthcare providers and enable consultations
✓Maintain your Health Vault for continuity of care
✓Verify healthcare provider credentials against NMC records
✓Process payments through eSewa and Khalti
✓Improve the app using anonymized, de-identified data only

🔒 What We NEVER Do

✗Never sell your personal or health data to any third party
✗Never share health data with advertisers or marketing companies
✗Never share your data with insurers or employers without your written consent
✗Never display advertising on the Platform

🛡️ Data Security

•All data transmitted using TLS/HTTPS encryption
•Health records encrypted at rest in Supabase
•Row-Level Security: users can only access their own data
•Private storage buckets for all medical documents

✊ Your Rights

•Access: download your records at any time via app Settings → My Data
•Correction: update incorrect information via Settings → Profile
•Deletion: permanently delete your account via Settings → Account
•Portability: export all data in JSON, PDF, or CSV format
•Object: opt out of research analytics and newsletters in Privacy Preferences
