Telemedicine Consent

Version 1.0 Updated: March 2026
By consenting during registration, you agree to receive healthcare consultations remotely via Swasthall. Please understand the real limitations before accepting.

✅ What You Are Consenting To

✓Receiving healthcare consultations remotely through Swasthall
✓Your health information being shared with the specific provider you consult
✓Use of Zego Cloud's encrypted video infrastructure for video calls
✓Consultation notes being stored in your Health Vault

⚠️ Real Limitations — Please Read Carefully

⚠A video call provider CANNOT physically examine you — no hands-on assessment
⚠Lab tests, X-rays, and physical procedures cannot be done remotely
⚠Video quality and connection issues may affect consultation quality
⚠Some conditions simply cannot be safely assessed by video

💊 No Medication Dispensing

✗Swasthall does NOT dispense, sell, or deliver medications
✗Pharmacist consultations are advice-only — pharmacists do not prescribe
•Doctor-issued prescriptions must be filled at a registered physical pharmacy

👤 Your Responsibilities

•Provide truthful, complete health information to your provider
•Be in a private, quiet location with good lighting for video calls
•Seek emergency care (call 102) if your condition is life-threatening
•Follow medical advice and attend recommended follow-ups

🚨 Telemedicine is NOT appropriate for emergencies. If you experience chest pain, unconsciousness, seizures, severe bleeding, stroke symptoms, overdose, or any life-threatening condition — STOP. Call 102 (Ambulance) or 100 (Police) immediately.
